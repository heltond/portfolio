Mapping Portfolio - Daniel Helton

This is a sample of some of the mapping projects I have completed as part of the New Maps Plus program at the University of Kentucky.

Earthquakes in the United States

A map displaying earthquakes in the United States in 2019. Seismic events are displayed and can be filtered by measured magnitude.

Tools: HTML, CSS, and Leaflet

Critical Habitats by Ecosystem

A map comparing critical habitats for endangered species to defined ecosystem classifications in the United States.

Tools: QGIS

Nuclear and Petroleum Power Plants

An interactive map that displays nuclear and petroleum power plants in the United States and shows output on hover.

Tools: HTML and Leaflet

Population Density by U.S. County

A color-coded map showing population density for every county in the United States.

Tools: QGIS

Amenities in Denver

An interactive map that displayes certain amenities in Denver and can be modified to only display those within a certain area.

Tools: HTML, Leaflet

Satellite Camera Locations

Tihs map shows the locations of satellite cameras in Kentucky and Tennessee. Images appear on hover.

Tools: HTML, JavaScript
